What Are Your Alternatives?

question every alternative to bitcoin - examine their fundamental vulnerabilities, their inevitable decay, their dependence on human systems. what asset can truly preserve value across generations, immune to both physical deterioration and human corruption?

The Illusion of Real Estate

the myth of real estate as the ultimate store of value dissolves under scrutiny:

• vulnerable to natural disasters and climate change
• subject to government seizure through eminent domain
• requires constant maintenance against decay
• exposed to local economic conditions
• immobile and undividable
• target for theft and vandalism
• dependent on stable property rights

The Debasement of Treasuries

government bonds offer the illusion of safety while guaranteeing loss of purchasing power:

when you hold treasuries, you're not earning a return - you're simply losing value more slowly than with cash. the interest rate is a mirage that barely obscures the constant debasement of the underlying currency.

The Index Fund Fallacy

the S&P 500 appears to offer steady returns, but look closer:

• returns mostly match monetary inflation
• companies constantly enter and exit
• dependent on continuous economic growth
• vulnerable to systemic financial risks
• requires stable political environment

The Corporate Mortality Rate

individual stocks face an even harder truth:

what company can you trust to exist in 100 years? in 1000 years? corporations die, industries become obsolete, innovations disrupt incumbents. betting on any single company is betting against the inevitable tide of change.

The Question of Generational Wealth

when thinking in generational timeframes, the requirements become clear:

• must be indestructible
• must be immutable
• must be incorruptible
• must be unseizable
• must be universally recognized
• must be eternally scarce

The One Eternal Asset

bitcoin stands alone in meeting these requirements. it is:

• immune to physical deterioration
• resistant to government control
• protected by mathematics
• secured by energy
• governed by unchangeable rules
• guaranteed to remain scarce

when you look for an asset that can truly last - that can survive natural disasters, outlast governments, resist corruption, and maintain its fundamental properties for generations - there is only one answer. bitcoin isn't just another option - it's the only option for true generational wealth preservation.
