Follow Best Practices

when following bitcoin voices:

• verify information from multiple sources
• be wary of investment advice
• focus on education over speculation
• understand potential conflicts of interest
• prioritize those who emphasize self-custody and sovereignty

remember: bitcoin is about removing trusted third parties - think critically and verify everything yourself.
