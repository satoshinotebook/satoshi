• floods destroy property
• fires consume wealth
• earthquakes demolish infrastructure
• storms wreck improvements
• climate change threatens assets

• institutions become corrupt
• custodians prove untrustworthy

• systems get captured

Monetary Decay

• inflation erodes purchasing power
• debasement dilutes value

• systemic risk threatens everything

• keys get misplaced

• human error is inevitable
